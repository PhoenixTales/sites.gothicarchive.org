Their music and their style of dress are strongly reminiscent of the Dark Ages. And their fire-spouting singer lives up to any magician in GOTHIC ...

In Extremo is Heavy Metal with hand-crafted bagpipes and other medieval instruments. The band sings in ancient Latin, Old ProvenVal, classical Spanish, Old Norwegian, Middle High German and modern German. This is not the most exceptional trait of the band, however. In Extremo also present a spectacular show, acting as prestidigitators, jugglers and fire-breathers.

As you will have guessed, In Extremo and GOTHIC speak the same language! What, then, was more natural than importing the band into the game? We all agreed very quickly that In Extremo would give a concert in GOTHIC!

First we scanned all of the band members. The next step was to motion-capture their "special moves" (such as fire-breathing etc.). This enables us to proudly present the unique stage show of In Extremo in GOTHIC. Be prepared for an exceptional experience! Here is a first glimpse of things to come:
