Our philosophy:

The core target of our enterprise has always been the development of international successful full-price titles.
What do we do in order to achieve this? We develop our games directed at main stream players.
This does not only imply an extremely simple user interface also suited for occasional players, but also audio and visual feedback for all the player's actions.

We are convinced that fascinating games do not have to be complicated. They should rather be very, very easy to learn and offer a manageable number of action possibilities. The combination of these possibilities and a clear reaction of the artificial world to all the player actions still result in enormous game depth: Quality beats quantity!
