Gothic
3D Action RPG
completed, released
15/03/2001
Egmont Interactive

In Gothic, the player takes on the role of a convict who must assert himself in an autonomous penal colony.

Since the colony has been separated from all influence from the outside world by a magic barrier, a merciless world has been established within it.
Magical ore is collected in the colony's mines, and only who controls the ore can control the colony.

Earning oneself a name in such surroundings is no easy task. Particularly since that isn't the only problem that players will have to confront in the world of Gothic.
