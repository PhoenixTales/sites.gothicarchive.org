In order to be prepared for the fast technical progress in the area of 3D games, the technology subsidiary CODECULT Software Research & Development GmbH was founded in July 1999. The core target of this enterprise is the development, servicing and distribution of a development environment for high tech 3D games. The achieved concentration of 12 specialists for the development of tomorrow's software technologies will further enhance the international competitiveness of both PHENOMEDIA and PIRANHA BYTES.

In February 2001, the mega project GOTHIC was finally concluded after a developing time of more than three years. When the game was introduced to the German games press, it received high assessments as well as 5 awards.
