Pages: short - detailed

Action RPG with a great freedom of action:
        Make up your own mind whether to kick the door down or search for the key, but learn to live with the consequences.
        You can open any door with the skills you have acquired, but you will have only yourself to blame for whatever lies behind the         door.

A new game with a new player character:
        Make your character a warrior, a thief, a magician or a psionic.
        Or combine these careers to suit yourself. Decide as you go along.

3rd person view:
        Weapons, equipment and talents appear on the player character at once. 

Facial animations:
        Lips move when characters talk and facial expressions change as the characters' moods do.

Simple handling:
        GOTHIC is self-explanatory and is played with just a few intuitive default keys.

More than 500 NPCs with their own memories:
        If an NPC is attacked, he will not forget it. In fact, he is very likely to tell his friends, and will certainly still remember it the next         time you meet. However, some other characters might view your action as a heroic deed.

Functioning social system:
        Make one enemy and all his friends will hate you, too. Help someone and his friends will automatically become your friends.

 Communicating with NPCs:
        All conversations are based on situations, i.e. you only learn what's important.

NPCs have a daily routine:
        Alls NPCs have lives of their own, night and day, no matter what you do.

Monsters cooperate:
        Watch out when attacking small monsters. They might have very big friends ...

Monsters have a private life:
        Monsters don't just wait around corners, ready for you to come along and kill them. You will meet them by chance, as your         paths cross. They hunt, they are hunted, they eat when they're hungry, they sleep ...
        NPCs go to work, interact with one another, eat, get drunk, smoke herbs, watch women dance ...

 A lot to be done:
        Complete tasks, solve riddles and fight for what you want. Find better weapons and improve your talents.

No endless statistics:
        All you need is a small overview. The rest is directly visible in the game.

No character generation at the beginning of the game:
        Simply launch GOTHIC and play.
        The game character is set up and improved during the game. You don't need to learn a rule system or have any skills that the         game doesn't explain.

In and outdoor worlds:
        The huge Upperworld grants access to many dark dungeons.
