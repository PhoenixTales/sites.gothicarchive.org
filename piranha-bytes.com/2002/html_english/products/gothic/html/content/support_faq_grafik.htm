Questions regarding graphic errors or problems with video cards in the full version:

The screen goes black/purple after the game is launched, there is nothing to be seen.

The following options must be activated in the video card's Direct 3D Settings (in the Control Panel, Display option) for video cards using the nVidia chipset (e.g. GeForce):
Enable Fog Table Emulation
Adjust Z-buffer depth


Known compatibility issues with certain video cards:

Whenever a problem occurs, check whether you have the latest reference drivers from the manufacturer of your chipset installed.
It may be worth your while testing whether Beta versions of drivers that have not yet been released solve the problem.

These are the most important links for downloading the latest drivers:

NVIDIA: www.nvidia.com (Section Support Center - Drivers)
ATI: www.ati.com (Section Drivers - Updates - PC Support)
MATROX: www.matrox.com (Section Video Cards - Support - Drivers)
3DFX: www.3dfx.com (Section Downloads)

In principle, the older your chipset and the less memory you have on your video card, the more the performance will suffer.

There are no known problems with any NVIDIA GeForce/TNT2 and 3Dfx VSA 100 (Voodoo4 and Voodoo5) cards, or with the Matrox G400 card. The ATI Radeon's faulty drivers lead to problems when the fog is activated (see your video card's settings). Chipsets with a lower performance level than TNT2, G200, ... are definitely likely to cause difficulties.
