Issue 5/2001


First-class high end RPG with spectacular graphics and a fantastic depth

Piranha Bytes have developed the German role playing highlight of the year 2001

If you like huge, demanding RPG worlds, if you're a fan of smart fights and up-to-date computer graphics, then Gothic is a must-have for your hard drive.

A meteorological firework unlike any you've ever seen in any other game tears across the sky. Obviously, all the other graphics of this game are also top quality.
