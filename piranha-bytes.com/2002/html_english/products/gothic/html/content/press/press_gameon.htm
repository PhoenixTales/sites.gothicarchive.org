Issue 5/2001


You just can't help falling in love with Gothic.
Jochen Gebauer

A grand feeling of being in a living, breathing world - all night long.

The Upper and Underworlds can easily hold their own  against Ultima 9, and the great weather effects and beautiful sunsets never cease to amaze us.

The various camps tempt you to investigate and play the game over and over again. The Upperworld is practically made for extensive exploration and the story is a superbly sinister feat.


Gothic - TOP OF GAMEON!
