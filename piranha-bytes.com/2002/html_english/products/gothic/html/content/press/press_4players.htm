Gothic is fascinating from the start, and the whirl of the gameplay will suck you deeper and deeper into the life of the Camps


A dense atmosphere, a lot of freedom of actions and an excellent quest structure make Gothic an exceptional game which is expected to set new role playing standards on a worldwide scale.

When I am drawn down into another world, grow oblivious to everything that goes on around me, and stare at my monitor, fascinated, then I know that the game I'm playing is exceptional.

Interaction with the NPCs and the freedom granted by the gameplay make Gothic an exemplary title.


Our compliments to Bochum - Gothic is simply fantastic!
