15/03/2001: Fanpage forums ...

First comments: Hi there. I've been playing this fantasy RPG from Germany for 6 hours now, and I'm floored. Gothic's atmosphere and mood are unlike anything I've experienced since Ultima Underworld or System Shock. The world's alive, the graphics are incredible. (I'm playing with Elsa's 3D Revelator Brille and it just looks sooooooo cool ...) The engine's very fast and has greatly improved since the demo [Athlon 1000 + Geforce2, 256 RAM, 1024*786 32 Bit, line of sight 300 (crazy!), stereo drivers activated (which weakens the frame rate by another 30%), and yet the Upperworld is smooth and completely fluid!! Phew! My compliments to Piranha Bytes' C programmers. The German voice-overs are professional and will take some beating in this genre. I also like the mouse control, and once you get used to the control as a whole, everything runs smoothly. The fight system isn't quite so cool as on Severance of Blade, but it's good. But the most unbelievably cool thing about the game is the world itself. It's captivatingly credible and sucks you in. I've been roaming about the mountains for hours, I've been watching and gathering experience. When you stand on one of those mountains and it starts to rain, you really get the shivers ... and you can practically smell the rain. As you may have noticed, I am greatly impressed, and I quite honestly wouldn't have believed that the boys and girls at Piranha Bytes would make it. Thanks for the game and I hope you have a lot of economic success. Regards from Lothar
